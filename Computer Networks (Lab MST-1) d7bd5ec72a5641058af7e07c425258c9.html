5. How you configure new IP address and Subnet Mask of your PC?

  1. From your Windows Desktop, click "Start"
  1. Click on “Control Panel”
  1. Click on “Network and Internet Connections
  1. Click on “Local Area Connection”
  1. Click “Properties” on the “Local Area Connection Status” panel.
  1. You should find a check mark already in the box next to “Internet Protocol (TCP/IP)”. Double Click “Internet Protocol (TCP/IP)”.
  1. Select "Obtain an IP Address automatically”
  1. Select “Obtain DNS server addresses automatically”
  1. Click the “OK” button
  1. Click the next “OK” button
  1. Close the “Network Connections” panel
